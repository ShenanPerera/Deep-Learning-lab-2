Question 1- What happens when the number of hidden nodes increase?

Answer - The accuracy improves significantly when the number of hidden nodes increase upto a certain point


Question 2- Can you explain the pattern of the accuracy when the hidden nodes increase?

Answer - The accuracy improves significantly when the number of hidden nodes increase. The highest accuracy is achieved with 5 hidden units (91.25%). 
but it slightly decreases for 20 and 50 hidden units. It means beyond a certain point, adding more hidden units does not lead to overfitting.
